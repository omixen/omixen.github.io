FAQ

How much food do I get?

The duo plan comes with two dishes, each rightly portioned for two people. The portion might vary depending on the dish.

How do I get the food?

If you are in the area, you can pick up the food starting at 6pm each day. Please properly wash and return the containers in the following pickup to be used for the next meal.

What kind of food are these?

We cook mostly Indonesian and Chinese-Indonesian cuisine. The combo provides dishes with the bold flavor of south asian spices and simple stir-fried vegetables for balanced diet.

These look yummy, How do I get started?

Email us your contact info. And we'll get in touch as soon as possible.
